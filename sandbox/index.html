Turn a conversation into a decision you can trust — fully local.

① Deliberate
Chat a decision through with your chosen model. e.g. "Should we ship the support beta?" — you weigh that 82% of tickets are FAQ-shaped against a privacy review flagging PII risk.
② Shape
✨ Suggest the structure, review, seal it. Your model drafts: decision = "ship to redacted tickets only", evidence = the 82% finding, surviving objection = "privacy risk remains." You edit, then Seal.

How the pieces work together
Prompt sandbox — where you deliberate

Chat a decision through with your chosen model. Load a system prompt from the Registry to set the model's role.

Registry — your versioned prompt library

Save, version, and grade reusable system prompts; load one into the sandbox to start a deliberation from a known footing.

ClisTa Protocol — shapes the decision

Turns the conversation into a validated, accountable decision log — question, decision, evidence, and surviving objections — checked for structural validity.

ThreadHub — notarizes & stores it

Signs and hash-chains the ClisTa log into a permanent, verifiable record. The Decisions view reads it back; cite a decision by its hash.
